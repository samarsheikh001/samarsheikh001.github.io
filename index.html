Performance Marketing, Own Startups & Side Projects

Meta Ads · Lead Gen · Hard-won lessons

Run my own campaigns end-to-end across personal startups and projects. The kind of operator experience you only get by spending real money, watching it work, and occasionally watching it not.

  • madedaily.in — drove ~100 qualified leads on ₹7,000 ad spend for a meal-subscription business; tight creative, tighter targeting.
